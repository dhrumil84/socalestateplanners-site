About Dhrumil
The Attorney Behind the Practice

Dhrumil Patel founded the Law Office of Dhrumil Patel to bring a more thoughtful, modern approach to estate planning for Southern California families. Licensed in both California and New York, he combines rigorous legal training with over fifteen years of enterprise analytics leadership at a Fortune 500 company.

Before dedicating his career to estate planning, Dhrumil built and led high-performance analytics teams responsible for turning complex data into executive-level strategy. That same discipline — understanding the full picture, stress-testing assumptions, and building systems with no gaps — is the foundation of every estate plan he creates.

Dhrumil's practice is built around the belief that estate planning should fit into your life, not the other way around. Every aspect of the client experience — from the initial consultation to document signing — runs on a secure, digital-first platform with evening and weekend availability as standard.

When he's not helping families protect their legacies, Dhrumil can be found spending time with his wife and two young children in Orange County.
